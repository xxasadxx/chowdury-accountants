Key Rules

  • All hires require MD approval before advertising
  • Right to work verified before start date
  • Signed employment contract required on or before Day 1

  • Probation: 3 months standard with formal review
  • Equal opportunities — all decisions based on merit

First Day Onboarding

  • Add to HMRC PAYE scheme and payroll
  • Set up email, systems and software access
  • Introduce to team — assign buddy for first 2 weeks
  • Provide staff handbook and key policies
  • Schedule 1-week and 1-month check-in with MD
